The numbers

Rules encoded here: horizontal depth-FOV coverage at the wall ≈ 1.41 × distance (2·tan 35.3°), vertical ≈ 1.15 × distance (2·tan 30°); time-of-flight depth is blind under 0.5 m and degrades past ~4.5 m (auto-calibration reads depth off the wall, so the wall itself must stay inside that range); autocal refuses projections narrower than 1.0 m or wider than 4.5 m. Squareness matters less than it looks — the ray→plane math is exact at any angle — but past ~10° the far side of the wall gets noticeably fewer, noisier depth pixels (this rig calibrated fine at 17° off-square; square it up when you mount it permanently). Keep ambient light on the people (pose runs on the color image), no direct sunlight, rigid mount, recalibrate after any bump — ./run-room.sh --calibrate --config=gesture-wall/room.kinect.json (~1 min).
