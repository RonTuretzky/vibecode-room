# Spoken idea scripts (e2e voice-injection fixtures)

One per line-block; injected via `say` → 16k WAV → /api/mic (see .context/mic-inject.ts).

## snowfall
I would like an app that takes in the amount of annual snowfall and then says
for how much time a family could drink that water, or how many people could
consume it for a year.

## twitter-charitable
I want a Chrome extension so that when I browse Twitter I can click a button
and all of the comments on my page become the most charitable, intellectually
honest interpretation of the comment, with research and studies backing the
claim.

## blind-dating
I want a dating app where after you match you don't know the person's name and
you don't see any of their pictures. You know you're attracted to them because
you matched, but you don't know who you matched with. It's blind dating inside
a dating app.
